How to delegate WFLR
  1. Prepare WFLRDelegation uses wrapped FLR. If you only hold FLR, wrap the amount first inside Flare Portal.
  2. Verify MirSFlrOpen this site's address page and compare the provider identity before any wallet signature.
  3. Open Flare PortalUse portal.flare.network, connect your wallet, then open the delegation screen.
  4. Select providerSearch for MirSFlr, choose it as your FTSO provider, and enter the WFLR amount.
  5. Review and signConfirm the wallet shows MirSFlr, never share a seed phrase, then sign the delegation.
  6. Wait one epochThe change normally starts from the next reward epoch. Check epochs before judging rewards.

How to stake FLR
  1. Prepare FLRStaking uses FLR, not WFLR. Keep enough free FLR for the amount and transaction fees.
  2. Check capacityFree space should be larger than your stake amount, and validator uptime should be healthy.
  3. Open Flare PortalUse portal.flare.network, connect your wallet, then open staking or validators.
  4. Select validatorSearch for MirSFlr and compare the validator identity with this site before selecting it.
  5. Choose amount and periodEnter FLR amount, select the staking period, review all details, then sign.
  6. Monitor stakeTrack free space, period end, and rewards on the validator metrics page after staking.
