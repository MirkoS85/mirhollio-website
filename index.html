Wallet quick notes
  • MetaMask: double-check network + contract prompts before approving.
  • Bifrost: locate delegation/FTSO section; verify provider identity via link.
  • Hardware wallets: confirm on-device display matches address/transaction details.
  • Epoch timing: changes apply from the next epoch; don‚Äôt panic if it‚Äôs not instant.
  • Fees: check provider fee and ensure you‚Äôre comfortable before confirming.
  • Security: never share seed phrases; beware of fake support DMs.
