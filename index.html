Understanding Delegation

Delegation is the process of entrusting your voting or staking power to a trusted validator or delegator. It allows you to participate in the network's governance and security even if you don't run a node yourself.

Delegating your FLR brings several benefits: earning staking rewards, supporting network security and decentralization, and maintaining a voice in governance.
